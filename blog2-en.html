Understanding MTCNA: Basics, Materials, and Exam Tips

MTCNA (MikroTik Certified Network Associate) is MikroTik’s entry-level certification...

In MTCNA, participants learn fundamental networking concepts...

Core MTCNA Topics

  • Introduction: Overview of RouterOS & RouterBoard.
  • Basic Configuration: IP addressing, DHCP, DNS.
  • Bridging & Switching: Connecting networks efficiently.
  • Routing: Static routing and basic routing concepts.
  • Wireless: Basic wireless network configuration.
  • Security: Firewall, NAT, and network protection.
  • QoS: Bandwidth management using Queues.
  • Tools: Torch, Packet Sniffer, Ping, Traceroute.
  • Management: Backup, restore, upgrade, and reset configuration.

Tips for Passing the MTCNA Exam

To succeed in the MTCNA exam, make sure you don’t just memorize theory but also practice a lot with real configurations...

Also, focus on topics that often appear on the exam such as IP configuration, NAT, basic firewall, and queues...
